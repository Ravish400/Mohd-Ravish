Research Projects

Vibration Damping of a Fast-Rotating Disc for an Electrodynamic Suspension System

Research Intern – IIT Kanpur, Department of Aerospace Engineering
  • Investigated vibration damping of a rotating disc used in an electrodynamic suspension system.
  • Conducted accelerometer-based vibration measurements and performed signal processing using MATLAB.
  • Designed a passive vibration isolation system to minimize vibration transmission.
✅ Outcome: 65% reduction in vibration transmission, improving system stability

Modelling and Analysis of Faults in Rolling Element Bearings

B.Tech Final Year Project – Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, AMU Aligarh
  • Developed an impact force-based vibration model for outer race defects and generated vibration response in MATLAB.
  • Introduced EDM-induced defects and conducted experiments on a Rolling Bearing Test Rig using accelerometer and DAQ system.
  • Validated the model by comparing experimental and simulation vibration signatures.

A Moving Mass Hitting a Stationary Spring–Mass System

Course Project: Mechanical Vibrations – Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, AMU Aligarh
  • Modeled a dynamic system involving a moving mass colliding with a spring–mass system.
  • Derived governing equations of motion using Newton's laws and a variational approach.
  • Solved numerically using 4th-order Runge-Kutta method in MATLAB for various initial velocities and mass configurations.
